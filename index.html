Pente Grammai

Instructions

1) The game supports a standard dice roll.

2) You have 5 blue pieces and the AI has 5 red pieces, all starting on the center line.

3) Roll the dice and click your blue piece to attempt a move.

4) The objective is to move your pieces from the center line to an edge of the board, following an anti‑clockwise direction.

When you choose "Left", your pieces will move upward one row at a time until they reach the top (row 0).

When you choose "Right", your pieces will move downward until they reach the bottom (row 4).

In any moved row (other than row 2) only one piece may occupy the highlighted cell at column 6.

Press [M] to return to the Main Menu.
