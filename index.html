Pente Grammai – Edge Jump Game

Instructions

1) The game supports a standard dice roll.

2) You have 5 blue pieces and the AI has 5 red pieces, all starting on the center line (line 2).

3) Roll the dice and click a blue piece to jump it to the edge.

4) If you choose "Left", your blue pieces are physically on the left half (cols 1–5) and will jump to the left edge (col 0); the AI’s red pieces are on the right half (cols 7–11) and jump to the right edge (col 11).

If you choose "Right", your blue pieces are on the right half (cols 7–11) and will jump to the right edge (col 11); the AI’s red pieces are on the left half (cols 1–5) and jump to the left edge (col 0).

5) Only one piece may occupy an edge cell (per row, except the starting center row).

Press [M] to return to the Main Menu.
