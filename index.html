Instructions

1) The game supports a standard dice roll.

2) I have 5 pieces and you have 5 pieces, all initially placed on the center line.

3) Roll the dice and click your piece to attempt a move.

4) The objective is to move your pieces from the center line to the opposite center line, following an anti‑clockwise direction.

5) Occupied spaces cannot be landed on. If you attempt to move a piece onto an occupied space, the move is illegal.
