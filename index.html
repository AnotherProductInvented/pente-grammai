Pente Grammai

Instructions

1) The game supports a standard dice roll.

2) You have 5 blue pieces and the AI has 5 red pieces, all starting on the center line.

3) Roll the dice and click your blue piece to attempt a move.

4) The objective is to move your pieces from the center line to an edge of the board following a counterclockwise route.

When you choose "Left", your pieces move upward one row at a time until they reach the top (row 0).

When you choose "Right", your pieces move downward until they reach the bottom (row 4).

In any moved row (other than row 2) only one piece may occupy the highlighted cell (yellow) at column 6.

Also, you cannot enter the goal cell (the designated cell on the opposite center line) if any piece remains in the start zone.

Press [M] to return to the Main Menu.
