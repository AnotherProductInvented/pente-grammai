Pente Grammai

Instructions

1) You have 5 pieces, and the AI has 5 pieces, all initially placed on the center line.

2) Roll the dice and click your piece to move it. Landing on a rosette grants an extra turn.

3) The objective is to move your pieces from the center line to the final line, following the Pente Grammai path.
