Penti Grammai (Five Lines)

Select Difficulty:

How to Play - Page 1

The game is called Penti Grammai, which means "Five Lines".

The board has 5 horizontal lines, each with 2 points (10 total).

You and the AI each start with 5 pieces.

How to Play - Page 2

Your pieces are blue circles on the left.

The AI's pieces are red circles on the right.

Click on one of your blue pieces to select it.

Then click a highlighted forward point to move.

How to Play - Page 3

You can move forward 1 or 2 points in a straight line.

You cannot move backward.

You can capture an opponent by landing on their piece.

First player to reach the end or remove all opponent pieces wins!
